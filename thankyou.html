"The choice isn't between success or failure. Failure is inevitable. The choice is between choosing risk and striving for greatness or risking nothing and being certain of mediocrity."

City-lover. Brazilian and Korean BBQ fanatic. Soon-to-be motivational speaker. Timothy Tan is a person whose passions are guided by his singular drive to improve himself from the bottom up. He believes that people are not fixed, but dynamic. But like all living beings, humans tend toward homeostasis. Therefore, he says, unless you give yourself a reason to change, you won't change. He believes that people often neglect the degree to which stress and suffering stimulate human growth - a critical fact of life.
